ABOUT US

The Foundation for Your Construction, Our Delivery.

We are Abu Bakar Traders, a professional cement distribution company. Our services are focused on delivering the best quality cement directly from the manufacturer to your shop. Our goal is to provide every customer with timely delivery of cement in perfect condition.

Our Mission

To ensure that every construction project has the right cement at the right time and place, so your work can continue without interruption.

Our Core Values

  • Fast Delivery: Our own vehicles ensure timely and efficient delivery.
  • Reliability: We are there when you need us, every time.
  • Customer Trust: Your trust is the foundation of our business.
  • Product Integrity: Delivering cement in its best possible condition.
